Altea Intelligence — Here to help with your mental health [BETA]

A calm space to talk about what's on your mind. Built by Jon Toledo Running on version 1.2 (latest) -- Currently in beta stage, still under development
Words of advice: Take things one step at a time. You don't have to handle everything today.

Important: Altea is not a crisis service and cannot respond to emergencies. If you are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services or a trusted adult.

Privacy: To protect everyone's privacy, messages aren’t stored by default. Avoid sharing personal identifying information.

In the UK, you can contact Samaritans (24/7) on 116 123 or text "SHOUT" to 85258. You can also visit samaritans.org.
